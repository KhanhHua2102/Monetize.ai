Using the Chatbot

The Monetize.ai chatbot provides personalized financial advisory services. Here are some tips on using the chatbot effectively:

  • Starting with an Empty Portfolio: You begin with an empty portfolio, and your risk tolerance is set to moderate by default. You can ask the chatbot any questions or seek advice related to investing, financial concepts, or stock markets.
    Example: "What are some common investing strategies?"
  • Updating Your Portfolio: Tell the chatbot when you buy or sell stocks. Provide details like the stock ticker, number of shares, date, and price.
    Example: "I bought 200 shares of Apple (AAPL) on 01/02/2020 at $300 per share."
  • Rebalancing Your Portfolio: Ask the chatbot to rebalance your portfolio using modern portfolio theory. It will adjust the weights of your stock holdings to match your desired risk tolerance.
    Example: "Can you rebalance my portfolio?"
  • Changing Risk Tolerance: Request the chatbot to change your preferred risk tolerance to low, moderate, or high.
    Example: "Please change my risk tolerance to high."
  • Ask for Recommendations: Inquire about stocks you are interested in or request general stock recommendations, the recommendation is based on analysts either buy, hold or sell the stock.
    Example: "Please give me stock recommendation of Tesla."
  • Get a stock price target: Ask the chatbot for a price target for a specific stock.
    Example: "What is the price target for AAPL?" "Bot: The current price target for AAPL is $178.88."

Viewing Portfolio and History

Monetize.ai provides features to view your portfolio and conversation history:

  • Portfolio: Check your investment portfolio, including balances, profits/losses, and other metrics. This information is stored in the Portfolio section for future reference.
  • History: Search for old conversations using keywords to quickly find past interactions with the chatbot.

Settings

In the settings page, you can access the following options:

  • Display User Information: View your account details and make changes if necessary.
  • Change User Information: Update your personal information such as name, email, and password.
  • Upload Profile Picture: Customize your profile by uploading a profile picture.
  • Change to Dark Mode: Switch to a dark mode theme for a different visual experience.
